Feeding Instructions

A single morning serving will allow your cat to nibble freely throughout the day. The serving size should be adjusted based on your cat's age and activity level. When introducing Meow Mix Original Choice cat food, gradually mix it with your cat’s current food over the course of 7–10 days.

Water is essential to your cat's good health. Always provide your cat with a bowl of fresh water.

Weight of Adult Cat (Over 1 Year)        Cups per Day*
5 - 9 lb        1/2 - 1 cup
10 - 14 lb        1 - 1-1/2 cups

*Based on a standard 8-oz measuring cup.

Kittens (Under 1 Year)
Feed 2 times the adult requirement.

Pregnant and Nursing Cats
Feed 2–4 times the adult requirement.
